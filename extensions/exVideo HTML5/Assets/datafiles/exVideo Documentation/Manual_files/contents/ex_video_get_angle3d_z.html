ex_video_get_angle3d_z

Gets the angle Z of a video in 3D space

Syntax

ex_video_get_angle3d_z() Returns the angle Z of a video in 3D space

Description

Gets the angle Z (rotation) of a video in 3D space.

Example

//Get the angle 3D Z and store it in variable _value
_value = ex_video_get_angle3d_z();

Minimum Supported Version

exVideo 1.0.0
