ex_video_get_angle3d_perspective

Gets the perspective of a video in 3D space

Syntax

ex_video_get_angle3d_perspective() Returns the perspective of a video in 3D space

Description

Gets the perspective of a video in 3D space used for rotation.

Example

//Get the 3D perspective and store it in variable _value
_value = ex_video_get_angle3d_perspective();

Minimum Supported Version

exVideo 1.0.0
